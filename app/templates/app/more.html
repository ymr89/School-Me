What makes School-Me better?

A lot. Different features, a different environment.



Security

Between permissions, authentication, and the ability to make boards private to communicate with students, School-Me's security features ensure that your data is safe.

Boards

Think of boards as tasks or assingments. Boards can be public or private, giving the opportunity for students to ask as much as they need. Help your classroom more than ever before.

Profiles

Every student has a profile with verified contact information that only board administrators can see. See who needs more help keeping the security, making for a community with privacy in mind.

Posts with threaded comments

Conversation can't happen when students and instructors are responding to one another in an unformatted way. Threaded comments make dialogue easier, leading the way to more meaningful learning.

File Upload

Upload your homework or explanation files. All the students will find the resources in one place. Keep it simple, keep it efficient.

Inbox

Allow your users to private message each other. Only authenticated users will be able to interact privately with more questions or extra clarification.
