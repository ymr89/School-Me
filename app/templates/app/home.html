What makes School-Me different?

School-Me was created by students, for students. The perfect way to communicate with your classroom.

Conversation

We know that sometimes classtime is not enough. Engage with the students, post explanations about homeworks, or just discuss the plans for the school year. Student will have the opportunity to engage outside the classroom.

Customizability

Your class in unique, this website is unique. Share your knowledge with students, get feedback from them. Creating a community has never been easier.

Comprehension

Be able to understand your students struggles. Comprehend their difficulties by their comments or feedback. Help the learning community in a different way.
